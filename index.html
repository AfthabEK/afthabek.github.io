NewPipe

Published on: 2023-08-17Author: Afthab E K
NewPipe is a lightweight frontend for popular streaming services like YouTube, SoundCloud, and PeerTube. It is completely ad-free and offers features like background playback and downloads, which are often restricted on other streaming platforms like the YouTube app. You don’t need an account for it as it parses the website of the streaming service to fetch the data we require. You can still subscribe to channels you like, and this data is locally stored, ensuring a secure and private user experience.

Librewolf

Published on: 2023-09-15Author: Afthab E K
Librewolf is a privacy-focused web browser that emphasizes user data protection and security. Built on the Firefox platform, Librewolf integrates various privacy-oriented extensions and settings by default, providing users with enhanced control over their online privacy. With features such as tracker blocking, cookie management, and secure connection protocols, Librewolf offers a secure and private browsing experience for users concerned about their online data.

Termux

Published on: 2023-10-02Author: Afthab E K
Termux is a powerful terminal emulator and Linux environment for Android devices, allowing users to access and run various Linux tools and utilities on their smartphones or tablets. With its extensive package collection and support for common development tools, Termux provides developers and enthusiasts with a versatile platform to perform various tasks, such as coding, scripting, and system administration, directly from their mobile devices.
